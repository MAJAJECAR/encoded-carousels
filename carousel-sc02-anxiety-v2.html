Anxiety is not caused by what is happening.

It is caused by what your brain expects to happen.

Psychologist Richard Lazarus found that emotional responses are generated by the subconscious interpretation of events, not the events themselves.

Two people. Same situation.

Neuroscientist Amy Arnsten found something important.

Stress physically impairs the prefrontal cortex. The part of the brain responsible for clear thinking, perspective, and rational judgment.

Anxiety reduces your capacity to think your way out of anxiety.

The loop is built into the biology.
