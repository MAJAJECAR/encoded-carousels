running your life were

Tier Two — Beliefs
Beliefs define what's
considered in the first place.

Patterns

The Invisible Filter
95%

of cognition runs subconsciously — every decision is filtered through beliefs the conscious mind never selected.

You can't debate a belief you can't see. It presents as how things are.

Action powered by what it gets back. Produces results — erodes the operator.

Expressing, building
Action powered by what it is. Produces results — and compounds the operator.
